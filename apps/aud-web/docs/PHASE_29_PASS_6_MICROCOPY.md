## âœï¸ Writing Guidelines

### Headlines & Headings
- **3â€“6 words max**
- **Concrete, not abstract**: "Plan your release timeline" > "Strategic planning workspace"
- **Benefit-focused**: "Get feedback on your pitch" > "Feedback system"
- **No punctuation** (unless question)

### Button Labels
- **1â€“3 words max**
- **Verb-first**: "Play Demo", "Back to Demos", "Replay Demo"
- **No articles**: "View Details" not "View The Details"
- **Action-oriented**: "Export Campaign" not "Campaign Export"

### Helper Text
- **1â€“2 short sentences max**
- **Be specific**: "Type `help` to see available commands" > "Get help"
- **Use examples** where helpful
- **No preamble**: Start with the point

### Empty States
- **Acknowledge the emptiness**: "No agents running yet"
- **Suggest next action**: "Run an agent to see results here"
- **Keep it brief**: 1-2 lines max

### Error Messages
- **Say what went wrong**: "Command not found: {command}"
- **Suggest how to fix**: "Type `help` for available commands"
- **Don't blame the user**: "That command isn't available" > "You entered an invalid command"

### Status Messages
- **Present tense for ongoing**: "Running...", "Exporting..."
- **Past tense for completed**: "Complete", "Exported"
- **Add ellipsis for in-progress**: "Coach is writing..."
- **Keep brief**: "Running..." not "Agent is currently running your request..."
