## What totalaud.io Is

totalaud.io is a calm, opinionated system that helps independent artists finish their music, understand what matters, and release with confidence.

It exists because most music doesn't fail because it's bad — it fails because no one explains what matters, feedback comes too late, and artists are overwhelmed by fragmented tools and advice.

**totalaud.io reduces cognitive, emotional, and creative friction.**

---

## The Core Problem

Indie artists don't lack tools. They lack:

- **Clear feedback** — A trusted second opinion before release
- **Prioritisation** — Knowing what actually matters for this track, this release
- **Context** — Remembering relationships and conversations across releases
- **Consistency** — A coherent story across every pitch, bio, and email

totalaud.io addresses all four.

---

## The Four Pillars

### 1. Finish

Help artists finish songs and releases with confidence.

Upload a track and receive finishing notes from multiple perspectives — like feedback from a producer, a mix engineer, a trusted listener, or someone with industry context.

No quality scores. No judgement. Just insight.

### 2. Release Planning

Help artists think in multi-week narratives, not single drops.

A visual timeline that encourages sequencing, momentum, and fewer-but-better releases. Connects creative decisions to release outcomes.

### 3. Leverage & Relationships

Help artists understand relationships as creative capital.

totalaud.io acts as memory — surfacing who matters, what was said, and what patterns emerge over time. Not a CRM. Not scraping. Just context.

### 4. Storytelling & Pitch

Help artists maintain a single source of narrative truth.

Consistency across playlist pitches, press emails, bios, and social copy. An editor, not a ghostwriter. A tone guardian, not an AI writer.

---

## Why This Exists

totalaud.io was built by someone who has been a lifelong listener, a band member, a DJ, a producer, a radio insider, and has worked in PR and plugging.

It exists because that experience made one thing clear: the hardest part of releasing music isn't the creative work. It's everything that comes after — knowing if it's ready, knowing who to reach out to, knowing how to describe it, knowing what to do next.

Most tools try to automate that away. totalaud.io tries to help artists think through it.

**Built from experience, not theory.**

---

## What totalaud.io Is NOT

- ❌ Not an "AI music critic"
- ❌ Not an auto-producer or mastering replacement
- ❌ Not a generic all-in-one SaaS dashboard
- ❌ Not a CRM or PR automation tool
- ❌ Not hype-driven or growth-hack oriented

We don't score music. We don't judge quality. We don't promise algorithmic success.

We help artists think clearly about what matters.

---

## How It Should Feel

Using totalaud.io should feel like working with someone who understands how much you care about your music.

Not a chatbot. Not a dashboard. Not a marketing tool.

A calm, respectful space to think through the hard questions before release.

## Technical Overview

For contributors and engineers, here's the high-level architecture:

- **Framework**: Next.js 15 (App Router)
- **Language**: TypeScript (strict mode)
- **Styling**: Tailwind CSS
- **State**: Zustand
- **Database**: Supabase
- **Animation**: Framer Motion
- **Build**: Turborepo + pnpm workspace
- **Deployment**: Railway

Detailed technical documentation is in `CLAUDE.md` and the `/docs` folder.
